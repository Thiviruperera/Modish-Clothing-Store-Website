Christiano Ronaldo’s Franck Muller Round

He might have previously been an ambassador for Jacob & Co, but CR7 recently upgraded his wrist game with
master of the tourbillon Franck Muller. His latest $1.5m timepiece is from the watchmaker’s Round collection
(points for inventive naming) and is fully set with baguette-cut diamonds and rubies across case, dial and bracelet.
It’s also impressive mechanically, with Franck Muller’s signature tourbillon.

     


Drake’s Richard Mille RM 69

As his penchant for diamond Stone Island pendants illustrates, Drake isn’t shy of a little bling.
Still, despite recent suggestions, Drake’s latest is not a Graff – and certainly not the Hallucinations watch –
but is instead a women’s RM 69 that, given his pull, Drake had the watchmaker size up for his wrist.
It reportedly costs around $750,000 which is in line with the rappers other Richard Mille, the RM 69 Erotic Tourbillon.

           


Angelina Jolie – Lorraine Schwartz Earrings

Angelina Jolie is no doubt one of the most beautiful women in the world and although in recent years she has been keeping a
lower profile with her esthetic she made a big statement in the 2009 Oscars wearing an astonishing pair
of Lorraine Schwartz emerald earrings. The emeralds were 115-carat, worth around $2.5 million and said to be part of Jolie's personal
collection.
